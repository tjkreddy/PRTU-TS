Frequently Asked Questions

What is PRTU-TS?

PRTU-TS stands for Progressive Recognised Teachers' Union - Telangana State. It is one of the largest teachers' unions in Telangana, representing over 72,000 government school teachers across the state. Founded in February 1971, the union has been at the forefront of educational reforms and teacher welfare initiatives for over five decades.

How can I become a member of PRTU-TS?

To become a member of PRTU-TS, you need to be a government school teacher in Telangana. You can apply by filling out the membership form available on our website or at your local PRTU office. The form should be submitted along with the membership fee to your mandal/district representative. After verification, you will receive your membership card.

What benefits do I get as a PRTU member?

As a PRTU member, you receive numerous benefits including representation in government negotiations, legal assistance for professional issues, access to professional development resources, regular updates through newsletters and bulletins, networking opportunities with other educators, and support during transfers, promotions, and other career milestones.

How does PRTU help in teacher transfers?

PRTU-TS actively participates in discussions with the government regarding teacher transfer policies. We provide guidance to members about the transfer process, eligibility criteria, and required documentation. Our representatives assist members in filing applications, addressing grievances, and ensuring fair implementation of transfer rules. We also organize workshops to help members understand the latest transfer guidelines.
