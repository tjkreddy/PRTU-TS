About PRTU-TS

Established in February 1971, the Progressive Recognised Teachers' Union – Telangana State (PRTU-TS) is one of the largest and most respected teachers' unions in Telangana, representing over 72,000 government school teachers.

Initially started as a union to represent the interests of Panchayati Raj teachers under the visionary leadership of pioneers like the late Sri Samal Yadagiri, PRTU-TS has grown into a powerful voice for educators across the state.

Union Strength

A strong community of 72,000+ dedicated teachers working together for common goals since 1971.

Advocacy

Advocating for better working conditions, salary revisions, transfers, promotions, and employment benefits.

Professional Development

Supporting teachers with resources, lesson plans, academic calendars, and continuous learning opportunities.

Political Representation

Active participation in Teachers' Legislative Council elections to ensure educators' voices are heard in policy decisions.
