PRTU-TS: Over Five Decades of Service

The Progressive Recognised Teachers' Union - Telangana State (PRTU-TS) has been serving the teaching community since its establishment in February 1971. For over 53 years, we have been at the forefront of advocating for teachers' rights, addressing issues related to salary revisions, transfers, promotions, and ensuring educational reforms that benefit both teachers and students.

Our union actively participates in political processes and contests elections for teachers' constituencies to ensure that the voice of educators is heard at all levels of governance. Recent elections have seen successful candidates like Pingili Sripal Reddy representing teachers' interests in the Legislative Council.

Union Publications

PRTU-TS publishes its own magazine and circulates newsletters and bulletins to keep its members informed about the latest developments, policy changes, and union activities. These publications are an essential resource for teachers to stay updated on matters affecting their profession.
