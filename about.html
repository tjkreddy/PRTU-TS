About PRTU-TS Suryapet

Who We Are

The Progressive Recognised Teachers' Union - Telangana State (PRTU-TS) is one of the largest and most respected teachers' unions in Telangana, representing over 72,000 government school teachers. Founded in February 1971, PRTU-TS has been at the forefront of educational reforms and teacher welfare initiatives for over five decades.

Initially started as a union to represent the interests of Panchayati Raj teachers under the visionary leadership of pioneers like the late Sri Samal Yadagiri, PRTU-TS has grown into a powerful voice for educators across the state. Our Suryapet district branch continues this legacy of advocacy and support for local teachers.

Our Mission

To empower teachers through collective representation, advocate for their rights and welfare, participate in political processes for teachers' constituencies, and contribute to the enhancement of the education system in Telangana. We work tirelessly on issues such as salary revisions, transfers, promotions, and other employment-related benefits.

Our Vision

A respected teaching profession where educators are valued, supported, and equipped to provide quality education to all students, with strong representation in political and policy-making bodies. We aim to be the most trusted platform for teachers to voice their concerns and secure improved working conditions.

Our History

PRTU-TS was established in February 1971 to address the growing need for organized representation of government teachers in the region. The union was founded under the visionary leadership of pioneers like the late Sri Samal Yadagiri, initially representing Panchayati Raj teachers.

Over the past 53 years, we have successfully advocated for improved working conditions, fair pay scales, and professional development opportunities for teachers across the state. Our union has also actively participated in contesting elections for teachers' constituencies, ensuring educators have representation in political forums, notably in the Teachers' Legislative Council elections.

Throughout our history, PRTU-TS has been instrumental in negotiating with government bodies on matters including salary revisions, revised government orders (GOs), and overall employment policies for teachers, building a reputation as a reliable representative of teachers' issues across Telangana.

Key Achievements

  • Successfully representing over 72,000 government school teachers across Telangana
  • Significant wins in Teachers' Legislative Council (MLC) elections, with candidates like Pingili Sripal Reddy securing victories
  • Effective advocacy leading to favorable revisions in salary structures and employment conditions
  • Establishment of robust digital resources including websites, newsletters, and social media presence
  • Regular publication of magazines, newsletters, and bulletins to keep members informed
  • Development of comprehensive professional support systems, including sharing of educational resources
  • Organizing numerous professional development workshops and seminars
  • Providing legal assistance to teachers facing professional challenges

Our Activities

Advocacy & Negotiations

Engaging with government bodies on matters including salary revisions, revised government orders (GOs), and overall employment policies for teachers.

Political Participation

Active involvement in Teachers' Legislative Council elections, supporting and sometimes fielding candidates like Pingili Sripal Reddy.

Publications

Publishing magazines, newsletters, and bulletins to keep members informed about policy changes and union activities.

Digital Resources

Maintaining digital platforms with downloadable resources such as government orders, policy documents, and various forms useful for teachers.
