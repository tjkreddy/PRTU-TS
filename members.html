Members Directory

Membership Benefits

  • Strong collective representation in negotiations with government bodies
  • Regular updates on policy changes affecting teachers
  • Legal assistance for professional challenges
  • Access to professional development resources
  • Support during transfers, promotions, and other career events
  • Network with fellow educators across Telangana
  • Participation in union activities and leadership opportunities
  • Subscription to PRTU publications and newsletters

How to Join PRTU

  1. Download and complete the membership application form from our website
  2. Submit the form to your local PRTU representative or mandal office
  3. Pay the annual membership fee (varies by category)
  4. Receive your membership card and welcome package
  5. Start participating in union activities and accessing member benefits

Join Our Growing Community

PRTU-TS represents over 72,000 government school teachers across Telangana. Our Suryapet district branch has a strong membership base committed to advancing the interests of teachers and education in the region.

By joining PRTU, you become part of one of the most influential teachers' unions in the state with over five decades of advocacy experience and accomplishments.
